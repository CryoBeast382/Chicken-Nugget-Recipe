Amazing Chicken Nuggets!

So you came here looking for some AMAZING chicken nuggets right? Well if you are you have certainly come to the right place. This is so amazing that there is no way you can pass it up!

  • Deep Fryer with Vegetable Oil
  • 1 package of chicken breasts (3 pack preferred)
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1-3 tablespoons of pepper
  • Garlic salt 1-3 tablespoons
  • 1/2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons onion powder
  • 1 tablespoons oregano leaves
  • 2 tablespoons Lawrys seasoned salt
  • 1-2 tablespoons taco seasoning (optional)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk

1. Heat vegetable oil in deep fryer to 370 degrees fahrenhiet. Cut chicken breasts into nugget sized pieces.

2. In a bowl mix together flour, breadcrumbs, pepper, garlic salt, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano leaves, Lawrys seasoned salt, and taco seasoning.

3. In a seperate bowl mix together milk and eggs.

4. Lay cut up breasts (6 pieces at a time) in a bowl with spices. Coat both sides. Do the same with egg and milk. Then refrigerate for 1 hour.

5. After refrigerated coat again in spices and deep fry for 4 minutes. Once deep fried let cool on paper plate or bag.

Now you are done! Thanks for going along with the recipe and enjoy your food!
